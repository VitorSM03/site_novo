The Cleveland Cavaliers, often referred to as the Cavs, are an American professional basketball team based in Cleveland.

Since 1994, the Cavs have played home games at Rocket Mortgage FieldHouse in downtown Cleveland, which is shared with the Cleveland Monsters of the American Hockey League.
Championships: 1 (2016)
Conference titles: 5 (2007, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018)
Division titles: 7 (1976, 2009, 2010, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018)
