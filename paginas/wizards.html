The Washington Wizards are an American professional basketball team based in Washington, D.C.

The team plays its home games at Capital One Arena, in the Chinatown neighborhood of Washington, D.C.

An arena they share with the Washington Capitals of the National Hockey League (NHL) and the Georgetown University men's basketball team.
Championships: 1 (1978)
Conference titles: 4 (1971, 1975, 1978, 1979)
Division titles: 8 (1969, 1971, 1972, 1973, 1974, 1975, 1979, 2017)
Retired numbers: 5 (10, 11, 25, 41, 45)
