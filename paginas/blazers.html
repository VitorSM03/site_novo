The Portland Trail Blazers is a basketball team based in Portland, Oregon.

The team played its home games in the Memorial Coliseum before moving to the Moda Center in 1995 (called the Rose Garden until 2013).

Retired jerseys: 1, 13, 14, 15, 20, 22, 30, 30, 32, 36, 45, 77.
Championships: 1 (1977)
Division titles: 6 (1978, 1991, 1992, 1999, 2015, 2018)
Conference titles: 3 (1977, 1990, 1992)
