NBA GEEK

The Toronto Raptors are a Canadian professional basketball team based in Toronto.

The team plays its home games at Scotiabank Arena, which it shares with the Toronto Maple Leafs of the National Hockey League (NHL).
Championships: 1 (2019)
Conference titles: 1 (2019)
Division titles: 7 (2007, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2018, 2019, 2020)
