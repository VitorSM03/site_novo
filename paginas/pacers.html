The Indiana Pacers are an American professional basketball team based in Indianapolis.

They play their home games at Gainbridge Fieldhouse.
Championships: 3 - ABA: 3 (1970, 1972, 1973)
Conference titles: 1 (2000)
Division titles: 9 - ABA: 3 (1969, 1970, 1971); NBA: 6 (1995, 1999, 2000, 2004, 2013, 2014)
